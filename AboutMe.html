Dtao the Elephant

Would you believe me if I told you I met this elephant three separate times? The owners of the sanctuary were from Issan, the region I lived in Thailand. We became fast friends, and it was my go-to place to take friends and family that came to visit me. For as massive as they are, elephants really are quite friendly. This one was named Dtao, which means star.
